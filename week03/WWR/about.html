Whitewater Adventures

Our mission is simple: to connect people with the raw majesty of wild rivers while providing unmatched safety and unforgettable thrills. Whether you are seeking a peaceful family float or a heart-pounding Class V rapid descent, our certified guides ensure your journey is safe, exhilarating, and deeply memorable.

History

Founded in 2004 by a small group of passionate river guides, Whitewater Adventures began with just two rafts and a shared dream. Over the last two decades, we have expanded our operations to cover over five major river systems across the country, serving thousands of adventure seekers every season while maintaining an impeccable safety record.
